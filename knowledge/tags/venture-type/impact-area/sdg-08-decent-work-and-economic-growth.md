# sdg-08-decent-work-and-economic-growth

## Definition
The `sdg-08-decent-work-and-economic-growth` tag identifies ventures focused on promoting sustained, inclusive and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment and decent work for all, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 8.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Create decent employment opportunities
- Support economic growth and development
- Address labor rights and working conditions
- Focus on skills development and training
- Promote entrepreneurship and innovation

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`
- Often combined with employment-model impact mechanism

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `employment-model`, `capacity-building`
