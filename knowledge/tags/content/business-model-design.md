# business-model-design

## Definition
The `business-model-design` tag identifies content focused on the process of designing, structuring, and defining a venture's business model using the Social Lean Canvas framework.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Guides users through initial canvas completion
- Explains business model design principles
- Provides frameworks for thinking about business models
- Contains instructional content on canvas sections

## Always Include When
- Content teaches how to fill in canvas sections
- Business model design methodologies are explained
- Initial venture ideation and structuring content

## Never Include When
- Content focuses on improving existing models (use improvement tags)
- Validation and testing content (use validation tags)
- Pure case study analysis without design guidance

## Content Examples
- Canvas section fill-in guides
- Business model design workshops
- Module 3.0 program content

## Related Tags
- Often combined with canvas section tags
- May include [concept](concept.md) for theoretical foundations
- Related to [template](template.md) for structured guidance
