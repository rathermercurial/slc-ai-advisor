# cross-subsidy

## Definition
The `cross-subsidy` tag identifies ventures that create impact by using revenue from paying customers to subsidize services for underserved populations who cannot afford full price.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Use revenue from one segment to subsidize another
- Operate "buy one, give one" or similar models
- Cross-subsidize services across customer segments
- Use market-rate sales to fund affordable services

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-mechanism`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-mechanism`
- Often paired with: `product-sales`, `service-fees`
- May combine with: various impact areas
