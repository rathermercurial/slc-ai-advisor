# job-customer-fit

## Definition
The `job-customer-fit` tag identifies content about validating that identified customer segments actually have the jobs-to-be-done the venture is targeting.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Defines job-customer fit validation methodologies
- Discusses techniques for confirming customer-problem alignment
- Provides frameworks for validating job existence
- Analyzes job-customer fit in case studies

## Always Include When
- Content focuses on validating customer-job alignment
- Problem validation with customer segments
- JTBD confirmation methodologies

## Never Include When
- General customer analysis without job validation focus
- Solution-focused content (use job-solution-fit)
- Product-market fit content (later stage)

## Required Combinations
- Often combined with venture-stage tags (idea-stage, early-stage)
- May include canvas-sections: [customer-model, jobs-to-be-done]

## Content Examples
- Job-customer fit validation frameworks
- Problem-customer alignment assessment
- JTBD validation techniques

## Related Tags
- Related to [jobs-to-be-done](canvas/jobs-to-be-done.md)
- Related to [customers](canvas/customers.md)
- Precedes [job-solution-fit](job-solution-fit.md) in validation sequence
- Often paired with customer-discovery
