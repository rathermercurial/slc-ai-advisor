# food-beverage

## Definition
The `food-beverage` tag identifies ventures in food processing, beverage production, nutrition products, and food technology.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Process and produce food products
- Manufacture beverages and drinks
- Develop nutrition and health foods
- Create food technology solutions

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `industry`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `industry`
- Often paired with: `sdg-02-zero-hunger`, `health`
- May combine with: `product-service-impact`
