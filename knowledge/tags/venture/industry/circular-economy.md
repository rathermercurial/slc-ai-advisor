# circular-economy

## Definition
The `circular-economy` tag identifies ventures focused on waste reduction, recycling, reuse, and circular business models.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Operate circular business models
- Focus on waste reduction and recycling
- Develop reuse and repurposing solutions
- Support circular economy principles

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `industry`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `industry`
- Often paired with: `waste`, `sdg-12-responsible-consumption-and-production`
- May combine with: `product-service-impact`, `systems-change`
