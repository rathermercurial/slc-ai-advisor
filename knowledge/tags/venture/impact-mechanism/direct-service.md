# direct-service

## Definition
The `direct-service` tag identifies ventures that create impact through direct provision of services to beneficiaries, typically addressing immediate needs.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Provide direct services to target populations
- Address immediate needs of beneficiaries
- Deliver services as primary impact mechanism
- Focus on direct service provision and delivery

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-mechanism`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-mechanism`
- Often paired with: `service-fees`, `gov-contract`
- May combine with: `healthcare`, `education`, `social services`
