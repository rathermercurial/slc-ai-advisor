# revenue

## Definition
The `revenue` tag identifies content about regular income generation from customers and other sources that sustains enterprise operations (distinct from one-time funding like grants).

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Defines revenue model development and optimization
- Discusses revenue stream diversification strategies
- Provides frameworks for sustainable income generation
- Analyzes revenue approaches across social enterprises

## Always Include When
- Content focuses on ongoing income generation models
- Revenue stream identification and development
- Revenue optimization and growth strategies
- Sustainable revenue model design

## Never Include When
- One-time funding content (grants, investment - use appropriate funding tags)
- Pricing content without revenue model context

## Required Combinations
- Must include appropriate canvas-sections hierarchy: [economic-model, revenue]

## Content Examples
- Revenue model frameworks
- Revenue stream diversification strategies
- Revenue optimization methodologies
- Social enterprise revenue approaches

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: [economic-model](../economic-model.md)
- Often appears with customers (revenue from customer types)
- Often paired with [costs](../costs/costs.md) (revenue-cost relationships)
- May combine with [channels](../channels/channels.md) (channels enable revenue)

## Key Concepts
- Recurring vs. transactional revenue
- Revenue stream diversification
- Unit economics and scalability
- Social enterprise revenue models
- Revenue sustainability
- Cross-subsidization models
