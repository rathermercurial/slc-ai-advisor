# sdg-14-life-below-water

## Definition
The `sdg-14-life-below-water` tag identifies ventures focused on conserving and sustainably using the oceans, seas and marine resources for sustainable development, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 14.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `oceans`, `oceans-and-coastal-zones`
