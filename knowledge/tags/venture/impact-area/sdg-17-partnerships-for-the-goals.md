# sdg-17-partnerships-for-the-goals

## Definition
The `sdg-17-partnerships-for-the-goals` tag identifies ventures focused on strengthening the means of implementation and revitalizing the global partnership for sustainable development, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 17.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `capacity-building`, `systems-change`
