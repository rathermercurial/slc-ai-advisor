# csr-sponsorship

## Definition
The `csr-sponsorship` tag identifies ventures that receive corporate social responsibility funding, sponsorship deals, or partnerships with businesses seeking social impact.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Receive corporate social responsibility funding
- Have corporate sponsorship partnerships
- Work with businesses on social impact initiatives
- Operate with corporate partnership funding

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `funding-source`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `funding-source`
- Often paired with: various legal structures
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
