# education

## Definition
The `education` tag identifies ventures providing learning services, educational technology, training, and skill development.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Provide educational services and programs
- Develop educational technology solutions
- Offer training and skill development
- Support educational access and quality

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `industry`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `industry`
- Often paired with: `education` impact area, `sdg-04-quality-education`
- May combine with: `direct-service`, `capacity-building`
