# sdg-09-industry-innovation-and-infrastructure

## Definition
The `sdg-09-industry-innovation-and-infrastructure` tag identifies ventures focused on building resilient infrastructure, promoting inclusive and sustainable industrialization and fostering innovation, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 9.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Develop infrastructure solutions
- Promote industrial innovation
- Support technology development and access
- Focus on sustainable industrialization
- Address infrastructure gaps

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `infrastructure`, `ict`, `research-innovation`
