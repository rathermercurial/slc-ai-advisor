# validation-and-iteration

## Definition
The `validation-and-iteration` tag identifies content about testing assumptions, validating business model hypotheses, and iterating based on feedback and evidence.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Describes validation methodologies
- Covers hypothesis testing approaches
- Explains iteration and pivot strategies
- Provides frameworks for learning from experiments

## Always Include When
- Content focuses on testing business model assumptions
- Validation frameworks are explained
- Iteration and pivot strategies are discussed
- Learning from experiments is the focus

## Never Include When
- Initial business model design (use [business-model-design](business-model-design.md))
- Pure case study without validation focus
- General improvement without testing emphasis

## Content Examples
- Validation experiment frameworks
- Pivot decision frameworks
- Customer feedback integration guides
- Module 5.0 strategy content
