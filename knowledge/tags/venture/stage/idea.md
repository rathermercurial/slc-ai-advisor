# idea-stage

## Definition
The `idea-stage` tag identifies content specifically relevant to idea-stage social enterprises focused on initial concept development, problem validation, and early framework thinking.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Addresses challenges specific to idea-stage ventures
- Provides guidance for initial concept development
- Discusses early problem validation approaches
- Analyzes idea-stage case studies and examples

## Always Include When
- Content specifically targets idea-stage venture needs
- Initial concept development frameworks
- Early problem validation methodologies
- Idea-stage canvas approaches

## Never Include When
- Content applies equally to all venture stages
- Content is specific to other stages (early, growth, scale)
- General ideation content not tied to venture development

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-stage-model`

## Content Examples
- Idea development and validation frameworks
- Early problem identification approaches
- Concept testing methodologies
- Idea-stage canvas templates

## Related Tags
- Parent tag: `venture-stage-model`
- Often appears with `purpose` (defining initial purpose)
- Often paired with `issue` (identifying core issue)
- May combine with `assumption-risk` (high uncertainty in idea stage)

## Stage Characteristics
- Initial concept development
- Problem validation and research
- Early framework thinking
- Concept testing and iteration
- Stakeholder discovery
- Initial purpose articulation
- High uncertainty and exploration
