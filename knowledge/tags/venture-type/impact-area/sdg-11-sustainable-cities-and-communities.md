# sdg-11-sustainable-cities-and-communities

## Definition
The `sdg-11-sustainable-cities-and-communities` tag identifies ventures focused on making cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient and sustainable, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 11.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `housing`, `logistics`, `infrastructure`
