# Industry Tags

This section defines all tags for categorizing ventures by their primary industry or sector focus. These tags help identify the market context and operational domain of social enterprises.

## Tag Structure

### Parent Tag
- `industry` - Must be included with all industry tags

### Industry Categories

#### Primary Industries
- `agriculture` - Farming, food production, agricultural innovation
- `oceans` - Marine resources, fisheries, ocean conservation

#### Manufacturing and Production
- `manufacturing` - Production of goods, industrial processes
- `apparel` - Clothing, textiles, fashion industry
- `food-beverage` - Food processing, beverages, nutrition products
- `circular-economy` - Waste reduction, recycling, reuse models

#### Energy and Environment
- `clean-energy` - Renewable energy, energy efficiency, sustainable power
- `water-sanitation` - Water access, treatment, sanitation services

#### Technology and Innovation
- `ict` - Information and communication technology
- `logistics` - Transportation, supply chain, delivery services

#### Human Services
- `healthcare` - Medical services, health products, wellness
- `education` - Learning, training, educational services
- `housing` - Shelter, real estate, accommodation

#### Financial and Professional Services
- `financial-services` - Banking, insurance, financial inclusion

#### Creative and Hospitality
- `creative-industries` - Arts, media, entertainment, design
- `tourism-hospitality` - Travel, accommodation, hospitality services

## Usage Guidelines

1. **Always include parent tag**: Use `[industry, specific-tag]`
2. **Primary industry**: Tag the main industry sector
3. **Cross-sector ventures**: Use multiple tags for ventures spanning industries
4. **Specificity**: Choose the most specific applicable industry tag

## Required Combinations

- Must include `venture-type` when using industry tags
- Must include `industry` parent tag
- Often combined with impact area tags to show industry-impact alignment

## Examples

```yaml
# Single industry focus
tags: [venture-type, industry, healthcare]

# Cross-sector venture
tags: [venture-type, industry, clean-energy, manufacturing]

# Industry with impact alignment
tags: [venture-type, industry, agriculture, impact-area, sdg-02-zero-hunger]
```

## Tag Definitions

### agriculture
Ventures focused on farming, food production, agricultural technology, and sustainable agriculture practices.

### apparel
Ventures in clothing, textiles, fashion, and related manufacturing or retail.

### circular-economy
Ventures focused on waste reduction, recycling, reuse, and circular business models.

### clean-energy
Ventures in renewable energy, energy efficiency, sustainable power generation and distribution.

### creative-industries
Ventures in arts, media, entertainment, design, and creative content production.

### education
Ventures providing learning services, educational technology, training, and skill development.

### financial-services
Ventures in banking, insurance, payments, and financial inclusion services.

### food-beverage
Ventures in food processing, beverage production, nutrition products, and food technology.

### healthcare
Ventures providing medical services, health products, wellness solutions, and healthcare technology.

### housing
Ventures in shelter provision, real estate, accommodation, and housing solutions.

### ict
Ventures in information and communication technology, software, and digital services.

### logistics
Ventures in transportation, supply chain management, delivery services, and distribution.

### manufacturing
Ventures in production of goods, industrial processes, and manufacturing innovation.

### oceans
Ventures focused on marine resources, fisheries, ocean conservation, and maritime industries.

### tourism-hospitality
Ventures in travel, accommodation, hospitality services, and tourism development.

### water-sanitation
Ventures focused on water access, treatment, sanitation services, and water management.
