# apparel

## Definition
The `apparel` tag identifies ventures in clothing, textiles, fashion industry, and related manufacturing or retail.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Manufacture clothing and textiles
- Operate in fashion and apparel retail
- Develop sustainable fashion solutions
- Focus on ethical clothing production

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `industry`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `industry`
- Often paired with: `employment-model`, `product-service-impact`
- May combine with: various impact areas
