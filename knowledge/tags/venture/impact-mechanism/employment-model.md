# employment-model

## Definition
The `employment-model` tag identifies ventures that create impact by providing jobs for target groups, often those facing barriers to employment.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Create employment opportunities for target populations
- Focus on jobs for marginalized or underserved groups
- Use employment as primary impact mechanism
- Address barriers to employment and inclusion

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-mechanism`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-mechanism`
- Often paired with: `sdg-08-decent-work-and-economic-growth`, `employment`
- May combine with: various industry tags
