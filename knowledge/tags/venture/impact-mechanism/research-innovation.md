# research-innovation

## Definition
The `research-innovation` tag identifies ventures that create impact by generating new knowledge, developing innovative technologies, or creating novel approaches to social problems.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Conduct research and development for social innovation
- Develop breakthrough technologies for social good
- Create new methodologies and approaches
- Generate knowledge to address social problems

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-mechanism`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-mechanism`
- Often paired with: `licensing-royalty`, `grant`
- May combine with: `ict`, `healthcare`, technical industries
