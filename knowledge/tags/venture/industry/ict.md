# ict

## Definition
The `ict` tag identifies ventures in information and communication technology, software, and digital services.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Develop software and digital solutions
- Provide ICT services and support
- Create technology platforms and systems
- Focus on digital innovation and access

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `industry`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `industry`
- Often paired with: `sdg-09-industry-innovation-and-infrastructure`
- May combine with: `product-service-impact`, `platform-commission`
