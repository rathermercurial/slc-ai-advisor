# membership-dues

## Definition
The `membership-dues` tag identifies ventures that generate revenue from membership organizations such as unions, cooperatives, associations, and community organizations.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Operate membership-based organizations
- Generate revenue from membership fees or dues
- Provide services primarily to members
- Follow cooperative or association models

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `revenue-source`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `revenue-source`
- Often paired with: `cooperative` legal structure
- May combine with: `capacity-building`, `direct-service`
