# l3c

## Definition
The `l3c` tag identifies ventures operating as Low-profit Limited Liability Companies - a US hybrid structure prioritizing social purpose while allowing limited profit distribution.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Have L3C legal status (US-specific)
- Prioritize social purpose over profit
- Have limited profit distribution
- Eligible for Program Related Investment

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `legal-structure`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `legal-structure`
- Often paired with: various funding sources
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
- Note: US-specific legal structure (select states)
