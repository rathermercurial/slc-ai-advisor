# housing

## Definition
The `housing` tag identifies ventures in shelter provision, real estate, accommodation, and housing solutions.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Provide housing and shelter solutions
- Develop affordable housing projects
- Offer accommodation services
- Focus on housing access and quality

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `industry`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `industry`
- Often paired with: `real-estate` impact area, `sdg-11-sustainable-cities-and-communities`
- May combine with: `direct-service`, `cross-subsidy`
