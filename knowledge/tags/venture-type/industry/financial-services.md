# financial-services

## Definition
The `financial-services` tag identifies ventures in banking, insurance, payments, and financial inclusion services.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Provide banking and financial services
- Develop financial technology solutions
- Focus on financial inclusion and access
- Offer insurance and payment services

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `industry`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `industry`
- Often paired with: `financial-services` impact area
- May combine with: `product-service-impact`, `platform-commission`
