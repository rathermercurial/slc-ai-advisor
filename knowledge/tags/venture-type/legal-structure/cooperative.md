# cooperative

## Definition
The `cooperative` tag identifies ventures operating as member-owned and democratically controlled organizations where members share ownership, control, and benefits.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Have member ownership structure
- Operate with democratic governance
- Share benefits among members
- Follow cooperative principles

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `legal-structure`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `legal-structure`
- Often paired with: `membership-dues` revenue source
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
