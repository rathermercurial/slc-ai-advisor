# long-term-outcomes

## Definition
The `long-term-outcomes` tag identifies content about sustained, significant changes that emerge from medium-term outcomes - typically occurring after 3+ years and representing lasting transformation in people, systems, or conditions.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Defines long-term outcome identification and measurement
- Discusses sustained transformation assessment
- Provides frameworks for lasting change evaluation
- Analyzes long-term impact attribution and validation

## Always Include When
- Content focuses on sustained transformation measurement
- Long-term change tracking methodologies
- Lasting impact assessment approaches
- Long-term change analysis

## Never Include When
- Short or medium-term outcome content (use specific outcome tags)
- Ultimate impact content (use `impact`)
- Immediate or intermediate results content

## Required Combinations
- Must include appropriate canvas-sections hierarchy: [impact-model, long-term-outcomes]

## Content Examples
- Long-term outcome measurement frameworks
- Sustained impact assessment methodologies
- Long-term change analysis approaches
- Sustained impact evaluation strategies
