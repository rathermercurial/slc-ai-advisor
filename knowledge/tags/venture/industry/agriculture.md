# agriculture

## Definition
The `agriculture` tag identifies ventures operating in farming, food production, and agricultural innovation sectors.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Engage in farming and agricultural production
- Develop agricultural technologies and innovation
- Support agricultural value chains
- Focus on sustainable agriculture practices

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `industry`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `industry`
- Often paired with: `agriculture` impact area, `sdg-02-zero-hunger`
- May combine with: `product-service-impact`, `employment-model`
