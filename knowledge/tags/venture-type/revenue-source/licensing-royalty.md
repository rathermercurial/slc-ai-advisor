# licensing-royalty

## Definition
The `licensing-royalty` tag identifies ventures that generate revenue from licensing intellectual property or franchising arrangements.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- License intellectual property to others
- Operate franchising business models
- Generate revenue from royalty agreements
- Monetize proprietary technologies or methodologies

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `revenue-source`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `revenue-source`
- Often paired with: `research-innovation`
- May combine with: `ict`, `manufacturing`
