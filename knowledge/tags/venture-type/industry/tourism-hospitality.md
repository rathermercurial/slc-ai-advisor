# tourism-hospitality

## Definition
The `tourism-hospitality` tag identifies ventures in travel, accommodation, hospitality services, and tourism development.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Provide tourism and travel services
- Operate accommodation and hospitality
- Develop sustainable tourism solutions
- Support community-based tourism

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `industry`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `industry`
- Often paired with: `employment-model`, `capacity-building`
- May combine with: various impact areas
