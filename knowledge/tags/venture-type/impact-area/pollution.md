# pollution

## Definition
The `pollution` tag identifies ventures focused on pollution prevention, reduction, and environmental remediation, based on IRIS+ impact themes.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `air`, `water`, `waste`
