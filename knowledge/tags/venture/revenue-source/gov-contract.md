# gov-contract

## Definition
The `gov-contract` tag identifies ventures that generate revenue from fee-for-service contracts with public agencies and government organizations.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Contract with government agencies
- Provide services to public sector organizations
- Generate revenue from public sector contracts
- Operate fee-for-service models with government

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `revenue-source`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `revenue-source`
- Often paired with: `direct-service`, `capacity-building`
- May combine with: various industry tags
