# oceans

## Definition
The `oceans` tag identifies ventures focused on marine resources, fisheries, ocean conservation, and maritime industries.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Work with marine resources and fisheries
- Focus on ocean conservation and protection
- Operate in maritime industries
- Develop ocean-based solutions

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `industry`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `industry`
- Often paired with: `sdg-14-life-below-water`, `oceans-and-coastal-zones`
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
