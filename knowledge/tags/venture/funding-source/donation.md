# donation

## Definition
The `donation` tag identifies ventures that receive philanthropic funding from individuals or institutions without expectation of financial return.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Receive individual or institutional donations
- Operate with charitable giving support
- Have donation-based funding models
- Rely on philanthropic contributions

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `funding-source`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `funding-source`
- Often paired with: `charity`, `nonprofit-inc` legal structures
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
