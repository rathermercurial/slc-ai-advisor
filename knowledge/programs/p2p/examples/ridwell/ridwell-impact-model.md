## Impact Model Overview
---

## Impact Chain

### Issue
Hard-to-recycle household items unnecessarily filling landfills due to system gaps and inconvenient disposal options.

### Participants
Household members, Ridwell staff, 200+ recycling/reuse partners, and broader communities benefiting from waste reduction.

### Activities
Household sorting, bi-weekly doorstep collection, warehouse processing, and transportation to specialized recycling/reuse partners.

### Short-Term Outcomes
Items diverted from trash, reduced disposal guilt, increased recycling awareness

### Medium-Term Outcomes
Millions of pounds diverted, expanded partner networks, normalized subscription waste services

### Long-Term Outcomes
Systemic waste reduction, stronger circular economy, policy changes supporting environmental responsibility

### Impact
A future where responsible disposal is as convenient as trash, supporting a circular economy with minimal waste.

Please note, this impact model has been compiled from publicly available data. It is a best estimate of this organization's impact model but information may be out of date and/or could differ from the organization's own description of their impact model.
