# diversity-and-inclusion

## Definition
The `diversity-and-inclusion` tag identifies ventures focused on promoting diversity, equity, inclusion, and addressing discrimination, based on IRIS+ impact themes.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `sdg-05-gender-equality`, `sdg-10-reduced-inequalities`
