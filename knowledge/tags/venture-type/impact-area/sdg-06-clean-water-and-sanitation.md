# sdg-06-clean-water-and-sanitation

## Definition
The `sdg-06-clean-water-and-sanitation` tag identifies ventures focused on ensuring availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 6.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Provide access to clean water and sanitation
- Develop water treatment and purification technologies
- Address water scarcity and management
- Focus on sanitation infrastructure and services
- Support water conservation and efficiency

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`
- Often combined with water-sanitation industry tag

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `water-sanitation`, `product-service-impact`
