# admin

## Definition
The `admin` tag identifies content related to administrative guidelines, processes, and metadata for managing and maintaining the Social Lean Canvas knowledge base.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Provides administrative guidelines and processes
- Documents knowledge base management procedures
- Includes metadata and organizational information
- Offers maintenance and update protocols

## Always Include When
- Content focuses on knowledge base administration
- Management procedures and guidelines
- Organizational and maintenance protocols
- Administrative documentation and metadata

## Never Include When
- Content about organizational strategy for ventures (use `organizational-strategy`)
- Procedural content for framework application (use appropriate framework tags)
- Content that mentions administration without focusing on it

## Required Combinations
- Often combined with `design` for administrative design content

## Content Examples
- Knowledge base management procedures
- Administrative guidelines and protocols
- Maintenance and update processes
- Organizational metadata and documentation

## Related Tags
- Often appears with `design` for design administration
- May combine with `prompt` for prompt administration
- Often paired with `design-note` for administrative design decisions

## Content Characteristics
- Administrative and procedural focus
- Knowledge base management orientation
- Organizational and maintenance protocols
- Metadata and documentation standards
- Process improvement and optimization
