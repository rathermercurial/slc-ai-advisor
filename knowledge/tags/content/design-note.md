# design-note

## Definition
The `design-note` tag identifies content that documents specific design decisions, rationale, and methodological approaches used in developing and maintaining the Social Lean Canvas framework.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Documents specific design decisions and rationale
- Explains design methodology and approaches
- Provides design implementation guidance
- Records design evolution and changes

## Always Include When
- Content documents specific design decisions
- Design methodology explanation and rationale
- Design implementation and evolution documentation
- Design process and decision-making records

## Never Include When
- General design content without specific decision focus
- Design templates or guidelines (use `design` or `template`)
- Content that references design without documenting decisions

## Required Combinations
- Must include `design`
- Often combined with `admin` for administrative design documentation

## Content Examples
- Design decision documentation and rationale
- Design methodology explanation and guidance
- Framework evolution and design changes
- Design implementation approaches and standards

## Related Tags
- Parent tag: `design`
- Often appears with `admin` for administrative design content
- May combine with `lexicon-entry` for design concept definitions

## Content Characteristics
- Specific design decision focus
- Methodological explanation and rationale
- Implementation guidance and standards
- Historical design evolution documentation
- Design process and decision-making insights
