# video-content

## Definition
The `video-content` tag identifies content that originated as video scripts, video transcripts, or is primarily designed to accompany video learning materials.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Contains video scripts or transcripts
- Accompanies video learning modules
- Is structured for video presentation format
- Originated from video production

## Always Include When
- Content is a video script
- Content is a transcript of video material
- Content accompanies video modules in programs

## Never Include When
- Pure text-based content not related to video
- Content that merely references videos
- General multimedia content without video focus

## Content Examples
- Program module video scripts (1.0 through 5.0)
- Video transcripts
- Video companion materials
- Learning module content

## Related Tags
- Often combined with program content tags
- May include canvas section tags for specific videos
- Related to [guide](guide.md) for instructional videos
