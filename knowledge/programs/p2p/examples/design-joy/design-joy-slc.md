## Canvas Structure

### Purpose
Eliminate design service uncertainty by providing reliable, premium-quality design work through predictable subscription pricing.

### Impact
*Note: Design Joy does not have a clearly defined social or environmental impact model. The venture operates primarily as a for-profit business service without explicit social or environmental mission statements.*

-----
### Customer Model

#### Solution
Unlimited design requests delivered via subscription with 48-hour average turnaround and Trello-based project management.

### Customer Model
#### Customers
Agencies, startups, and established businesses in the SaaS/technology space requiring reliable, ongoing design services.

**Early Adopters:**
Early SaaS startups and agencies discovered through Product Hunt launch and founder's network in technology space.

#### Jobs to be Done
Provide predictable, high-quality design capacity without the risks and overhead of traditional design resources.

**Existing Alternatives:**
Freelance platforms, traditional agencies, in-house hiring, or lower-cost design subscription competitors.

#### Unique Value Proposition
Premium design quality with guaranteed 48-hour delivery, unlimited revisions, and direct founder involvement.

#### Solution
Unlimited design requests delivered via subscription with 48-hour average turnaround and Trello-based project management.

-----
### Economic Model

#### Channels
Social media marketing, word-of-mouth referrals, educational content, and digital project management platforms.

#### Costs
Software subscriptions, payment processing fees, and minimal operational overhead as a one-person business.

#### Revenue
Monthly subscriptions starting at $4,995, generating $150,000+ monthly recurring revenue.

#### Advantage
First-mover advantage in subscription design, unmatched speed-quality combination, and premium brand positioning through founder expertise.

------
#### Key Metrics
Monthly recurring revenue, 48-hour delivery success rate, client retention, and subscription capacity utilization.
