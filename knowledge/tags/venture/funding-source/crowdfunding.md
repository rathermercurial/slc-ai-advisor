# crowdfunding

## Definition
The `crowdfunding` tag identifies ventures that raise funding from large numbers of small contributors, typically through online platforms.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Use online crowdfunding platforms
- Raise funds from many small contributors
- Operate with community-based funding
- Use platform-based funding approaches

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `funding-source`

- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `funding-source`
- Often paired with: various legal structures
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
