# manufacturing

## Definition
The `manufacturing` tag identifies ventures in production of goods, industrial processes, and manufacturing innovation.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Manufacture products and goods
- Develop manufacturing technologies
- Focus on sustainable production processes
- Support manufacturing value chains

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `industry`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `industry`
- Often paired with: `sdg-09-industry-innovation-and-infrastructure`
- May combine with: `product-service-impact`, `employment-model`
