# sdg-04-quality-education

## Definition
The `sdg-04-quality-education` tag identifies ventures focused on ensuring inclusive and equitable quality education and promoting lifelong learning opportunities for all, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 4.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Provide access to quality education at all levels
- Develop educational technology and innovation
- Support teacher training and capacity building
- Address educational inequality and inclusion
- Promote lifelong learning and skills development
- Improve educational infrastructure and resources
- Focus on literacy and numeracy development

## Always Include When
- Educational service providers and institutions
- Educational technology and digital learning platforms
- Teacher training and professional development programs
- Educational access initiatives for underserved populations
- Skills development and vocational training
- Literacy and numeracy programs
- Educational infrastructure development

## Never Include When
- Commercial training without clear educational mission
- Entertainment or media not focused on learning outcomes
- Corporate training programs without broader educational impact
- Educational content not addressing SDG 4 targets

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`
- Often combined with `education` industry tag
- May combine with specific impact mechanisms like `direct-service` or `capacity-building`

## Content Examples
- Online learning platforms for underserved communities
- Teacher training and certification programs
- Educational technology for developing countries
- Literacy programs for adults and children
- Vocational skills training initiatives
- Inclusive education for students with disabilities

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Industry alignment: `education`, `ict`
- Impact mechanisms: `direct-service`, `capacity-building`, `product-service-impact`
- Often paired with: `employment-model`, `cross-subsidy`

## Key Concepts
- Educational access and equity
- Quality learning outcomes
- Teacher capacity and training
- Educational technology and innovation
- Lifelong learning opportunities
- Skills development and employability

## SDG 4 Targets Alignment
- 4.1: Free, equitable, and quality primary and secondary education
- 4.2: Access to quality early childhood development and education
- 4.3: Equal access to affordable technical, vocational, and higher education
- 4.4: Increase number of people with relevant skills for employment
- 4.5: Eliminate gender disparities and ensure equal access for vulnerable populations
- 4.6: Ensure all youth and adults achieve literacy and numeracy
- 4.7: Ensure learners acquire knowledge for sustainable development
