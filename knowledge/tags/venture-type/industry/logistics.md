# logistics

## Definition
The `logistics` tag identifies ventures in transportation, supply chain management, delivery services, and distribution.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Provide transportation and delivery services
- Manage supply chains and logistics
- Develop distribution solutions
- Focus on logistics innovation and efficiency

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `industry`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `industry`
- Often paired with: `sdg-11-sustainable-cities-and-communities`
- May combine with: `product-service-impact`, `platform-commission`
