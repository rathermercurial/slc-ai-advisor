## Impact Model

### Issue
The climate and ecological crisis driven by the apparel industry's 10% contribution to global carbon emissions.

### Participants
The environment, customers, employees, supply chain workers, grassroots NGOs, and indigenous communities.

### Activities
Donating 1% of sales to environmental causes, advocacy campaigns, and innovating sustainable materials and practices.

### Short-Term Outcomes
Hundreds of environmental initiatives funded annually and immediate emission reductions from recycled materials.

### Medium-Term Outcomes
Industry adoption of sustainable practices and strengthened environmental organizations winning conservation victories.

### Long-Term Outcomes
Transformation of capitalism to prioritize planet over profit across the business world.

### Impact
A thriving planet with stabilized climate and intact ecosystems for future generations.
