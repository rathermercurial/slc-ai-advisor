# Solution

**Quick Definition**: What your enterprise actually does - your product or service that delivers customer value and creates impact.

**Key Insight**: Your solution is the vehicle that connects everything together - it delivers your unique value proposition to customers, addresses their jobs to be done, and creates the impact outlined in your impact model.
