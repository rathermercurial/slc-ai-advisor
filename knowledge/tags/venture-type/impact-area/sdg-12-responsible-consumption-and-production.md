# sdg-12-responsible-consumption-and-production

## Definition
The `sdg-12-responsible-consumption-and-production` tag identifies ventures focused on ensuring sustainable consumption and production patterns, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 12.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `circular-economy`, `manufacturing`, `waste`
