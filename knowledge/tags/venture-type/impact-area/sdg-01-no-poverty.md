# sdg-01-no-poverty

## Definition
The `sdg-01-no-poverty` tag identifies ventures focused on ending poverty in all its forms everywhere, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 1.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Address income poverty and economic empowerment
- Provide access to basic services and resources
- Support poverty alleviation programs
- Focus on economic inclusion and livelihood creation
- Address extreme poverty and vulnerability

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`
- Often combined with employment, financial services, or capacity building mechanisms

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `employment-model`, `financial-services`, `capacity-building`
