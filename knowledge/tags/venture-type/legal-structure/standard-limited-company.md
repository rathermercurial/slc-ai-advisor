# standard-limited-company

## Definition
The `standard-limited-company` tag identifies ventures operating as traditional for-profit limited companies or corporations without specific social purpose requirements.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Have traditional for-profit incorporation
- Operate under standard corporate law
- Focus primarily on shareholder returns
- Do not have legal social purpose requirements

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `legal-structure`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `legal-structure`
- Often paired with: `impact-equity`, `bootstrapped` funding sources
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
