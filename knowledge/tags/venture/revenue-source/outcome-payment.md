# outcome-payment

## Definition
The `outcome-payment` tag identifies ventures that generate revenue tied to achieving specific social or environmental outcomes, including Social Impact Bonds (SIBs), Development Impact Bonds (DIBs), and Pay-for-Success contracts.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Participate in impact bond structures
- Generate revenue based on outcome achievement
- Operate pay-for-success models
- Have payment tied to measurable social results

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `revenue-source`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `revenue-source`
- Often paired with: outcome-focused impact mechanisms
- May combine with: various impact areas
