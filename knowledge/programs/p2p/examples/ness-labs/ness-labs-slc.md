# Ness Labs - Social Lean Canvas

## Canvas Overview
---

**Version:** 1.0

## Canvas Structure

### Purpose
Empower knowledge workers to pursue sustainable, science-backed productivity and lifelong learning without sacrificing mental health.

### Impact
Normalize sustainable, research-informed productivity and lifelong learning across individuals and workplaces.
*[See full Impact Model](ness-labs-impact-model.md)*

-----
### Customer Model

#### Solution
A newsletter-led, membership-based learning community with courses, events, and perks, amplified by a trade-published book.

### Customer Model
#### Customers
**Members:** Knowledge workers and creators seeking courses, events, and community.
**Readers:** Newsletter subscribers exploring science-backed articles.
**Sponsors:** Productivity/learning brands targeting engaged, niche audiences.
**Book readers:** General audience discovering the brand.

**Early Adopters:**
Indie makers and PKM communities (note-taking/knowledge management), newsletter-native learners, neuroscience-curious readers.

#### Jobs to be Done
**Members:** Turn science into habits; accountability; like-minded community.
**Readers:** Curated, applicable insights.
**Sponsors:** Trusted reach to engaged learners/creators.

**Existing Alternatives:**
Books, blogs, and generic communities; coaching; larger newsletters/podcasts; paid social; or "do nothing."

#### Unique Value Proposition
**Members/readers:** Neuroscience-grounded, mindful productivity with courses, events, and community support.
**Sponsors:** Trusted access to a high-intent, tool-literate audience.

#### Solution
A newsletter-led, membership-based learning community with courses, events, and perks, amplified by a trade-published book.

-----
### Economic Model

#### Channels
SEO content + newsletter + book for acquisition; website, email, members' area, events, and recordings for delivery.

#### Costs
Tooling/hosting, membership/payment platforms, video/event software, contractor/editorial support, and community operations.

#### Revenue
Membership subscriptions; newsletter sponsorships; book royalties (and indirect speaking/PR benefits).

#### Advantage
Authority + audience + low-price membership; compounding content library and ecosystem flywheel.

------
#### Key Metrics
Newsletter growth/engagement; site traffic; free→paid conversion; active members; event/course engagement; sponsor performance; book sales.
