# advertising-sponsorship

## Definition
The `advertising-sponsorship` tag identifies ventures that generate revenue from advertising placements, content sponsorship, and media monetization.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Monetize through advertising placements
- Generate revenue from content sponsorships
- Operate media and content platforms
- Provide advertising and marketing services

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `revenue-source`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `revenue-source`
- Often paired with: `creative-industries`, `ict`
- May combine with: `capacity-building`, `policy-advocacy`
