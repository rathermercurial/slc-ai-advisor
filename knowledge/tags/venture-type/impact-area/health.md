# health

## Definition
The `health` tag identifies ventures focused on health outcomes, healthcare access, and health systems strengthening, based on IRIS+ impact themes.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `sdg-03-good-health-and-well-being`, `healthcare`
