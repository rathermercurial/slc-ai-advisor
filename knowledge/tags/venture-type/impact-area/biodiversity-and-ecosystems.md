# biodiversity-and-ecosystems

## Definition
The `biodiversity-and-ecosystems` tag identifies ventures focused on protecting and restoring biodiversity, ecosystem conservation, and natural habitat preservation, based on IRIS+ impact themes.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `sdg-15-life-on-land`, `land`
