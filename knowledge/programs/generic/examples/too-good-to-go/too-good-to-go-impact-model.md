## Impact Chain

### Issue
Global food waste crisis: one-third of food produced is wasted, causing 10% of greenhouse gas emissions and massive environmental degradation.

### Participants
Food businesses, environmentally conscious consumers, food service workers, local communities, environmental organizations, and policymakers working to reduce food waste.

### Activities
Surplus food redistribution, impact tracking, community building, education campaigns, policy advocacy, industry partnerships, and technology innovation for systemic change.

### Short-Term Outcomes
Direct food waste reduction, individual behavior change, business revenue recovery, measurable environmental impact, and increased community awareness.

### Medium-Term Outcomes
Scaling impact, industry standard changes, cultural shifts toward sustainability, business model innovation, and supportive policy development.

### Long-Term Outcomes
Systemic food system transformation, significant climate impact, permanent consumer behavior change, industry-wide operational improvements, and global awareness.

### Impact
A world without food waste where businesses, consumers, and systems prioritize environmental sustainability, contributing significantly to global climate goals.
