# growth-stage

## Definition
The `growth-stage` tag identifies content specifically relevant to growth-stage social enterprises focused on scaling proven models, expanding reach, and optimizing operations.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Addresses challenges specific to growth-stage ventures
- Provides guidance for scaling and expansion
- Discusses optimization and efficiency approaches
- Analyzes growth-stage case studies and examples

## Always Include When
- Content specifically targets growth-stage venture needs
- Scaling and expansion frameworks
- Operational optimization methodologies
- Growth-stage canvas refinement approaches

## Never Include When
- Content applies equally to all venture stages
- Content is specific to other stages (idea, early, scale)
- General growth content not tied to social venture context

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-stage-model`

## Content Examples
- Scaling frameworks and methodologies
- Operational optimization approaches
- Growth-stage impact measurement
- Expansion strategy frameworks

## Related Tags
- Parent tag: `venture-stage-model`
- Often appears with `channels` (scaling customer acquisition)
- Often paired with `revenue` (revenue optimization and growth)
- May combine with `key-metrics` (growth measurement)

## Stage Characteristics
- Scaling proven business models
- Expanding geographic or market reach
- Optimizing operations and processes
- Refining and improving canvas elements
- Building organizational capabilities
- Increasing impact scale
- Managing growth challenges
