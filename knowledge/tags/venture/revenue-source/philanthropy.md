# philanthropy

## Definition
The `philanthropy` tag identifies ventures that generate revenue from one-off or recurring donations (distinct from grants which are typically for specific projects).

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Receive individual or institutional donations
- Generate revenue from charitable giving
- Rely on philanthropic contributions
- Operate donation-based revenue models

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `revenue-source`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `revenue-source`
- Often paired with: `charity`, `nonprofit-inc` legal structures
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
