# clean-energy

## Definition
The `clean-energy` tag identifies ventures operating in renewable energy, energy efficiency, sustainable power generation and distribution sectors.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Develop or deploy renewable energy technologies
- Provide energy efficiency solutions and services
- Focus on sustainable power generation
- Address energy access in underserved communities
- Operate clean energy distribution systems
- Support clean energy infrastructure development

## Always Include When
- Solar, wind, hydro, and other renewable energy projects
- Energy efficiency technology and services
- Clean energy access initiatives
- Sustainable energy infrastructure development
- Energy storage and grid solutions
- Clean energy finance and investment

## Never Include When
- Traditional fossil fuel energy projects
- General technology not specifically energy-focused
- Energy-adjacent sectors without clear clean energy focus
- Industrial projects not primarily energy-focused

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `industry`
- Often combined with climate and energy impact area tags
- May combine with various revenue and funding source tags

## Content Examples
- Solar energy systems for rural electrification
- Energy efficiency services for low-income housing
- Micro-grid solutions for remote communities
- Clean cooking technologies and distribution
- Energy storage and battery technologies
- Clean energy financing and leasing models

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `industry`
- Impact alignment: `sdg-07-affordable-and-clean-energy`, `energy`, `climate`
- Revenue sources: `product-sales`, `service-fees`, `outcome-payment`
- Impact mechanisms: `product-service-impact`, `direct-service`

## Energy Sectors
- **Solar Energy**: Photovoltaic and solar thermal systems
- **Wind Energy**: Wind turbine and wind farm development
- **Hydroelectric**: Small-scale and micro-hydro projects
- **Energy Storage**: Battery and storage technology solutions
- **Energy Efficiency**: Building and industrial efficiency solutions
- **Energy Access**: Off-grid and distributed energy solutions

## Key Considerations
- Technology performance and reliability
- Economic viability and affordability
- Environmental impact and sustainability
- Energy access and energy poverty
- Grid integration and infrastructure
- Policy and regulatory environment
- Climate impact and carbon reduction
