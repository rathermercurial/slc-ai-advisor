## Impact Chain

### Issue
Widespread career dissatisfaction among accomplished professionals trapped in conventional success paths prioritizing external achievement over fulfillment.

### Participants
Career questioners, their families, employers, professional communities, and society benefiting from more authentic work alignment.

### Activities
Publishing books, teaching courses, hosting conversations, coaching individuals, speaking, and building communities around alternative work paths.

### Short-Term Outcomes
New perspectives on work, increased confidence to question conventional paths.

### Medium-Term Outcomes
Successful transitions to fulfilling work, sustainable alternative income streams.

### Long-Term Outcomes
Cultural shift toward valuing fulfillment, acceptance of alternative career paths.

### Impact
A world where people pursue work that makes them feel alive, creating better talent allocation and healthier communities.
