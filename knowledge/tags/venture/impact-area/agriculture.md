# agriculture

## Definition
The `agriculture` tag identifies ventures focused on agricultural systems, farming practices, food production, and rural development impacts, based on IRIS+ impact themes.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `agriculture` industry tag, `sdg-02-zero-hunger`
