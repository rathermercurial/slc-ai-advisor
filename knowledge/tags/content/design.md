# design

## Definition
The `design` tag identifies content about design decisions, methodologies, and approaches used in the Social Lean Canvas framework, including visual design, information architecture, and user experience considerations.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Explains design decisions and methodologies
- Discusses framework design principles and approaches
- Provides design guidance and standards
- Analyzes design effectiveness and implementation

## Always Include When
- Content focuses on design principles and decisions
- Framework design methodology and rationale
- Design standards and guidelines
- Visual and information design approaches

## Never Include When
- Content about business design or strategy (use `strategy-model`)
- Solution design content (use `solution`)
- Content that mentions design without focusing on it

## Required Combinations
- Often combined with specific design sub-component tags
- May include `admin` for administrative design content

## Content Examples
- Framework design principles and methodology
- Visual design standards and guidelines
- Information architecture and organization
- User experience design approaches

## Related Tags
- Sub-component tags: `design-note`, `prompt`, `admin`
- Often appears with `lexicon-entry` for design terminology
- May combine with `template` for design templates
