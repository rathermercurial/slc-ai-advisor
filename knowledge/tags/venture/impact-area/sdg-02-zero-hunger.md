# sdg-02-zero-hunger

## Definition
The `sdg-02-zero-hunger` tag identifies ventures focused on ending hunger, achieving food security, improving nutrition, and promoting sustainable agriculture, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 2.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Address food insecurity and hunger
- Improve nutrition and food quality
- Promote sustainable agriculture practices
- Enhance agricultural productivity
- Support smallholder farmers
- Reduce food waste and loss
- Strengthen food systems resilience

## Always Include When
- Ventures directly addressing hunger or malnutrition
- Agricultural technology and innovation
- Food security programs and initiatives
- Sustainable farming and agricultural practices
- Nutrition-focused products or services
- Food access and distribution solutions

## Never Include When
- General food businesses without clear hunger/nutrition focus
- Luxury or premium food products without social mission
- Agricultural ventures focused solely on commercial gain
- Food-related content not addressing SDG 2 targets

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`
- Often combined with `agriculture` or `food-beverage` industry tags
- May combine with specific impact mechanisms like `product-service-impact`

## Content Examples
- Agricultural technology for smallholder farmers
- Nutrition-focused food products for underserved populations
- Food security and distribution programs
- Sustainable agriculture practices and training
- Anti-hunger advocacy and policy initiatives

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Industry alignment: `agriculture`, `food-beverage`
- Impact mechanisms: `product-service-impact`, `direct-service`, `capacity-building`
- Often paired with: `employment-model`, `cross-subsidy`

## Key Concepts
- Food security and access
- Nutritional outcomes and health
- Sustainable agricultural practices
- Smallholder farmer support
- Food system resilience
- Hunger elimination strategies

## SDG 2 Targets Alignment
- 2.1: End hunger and ensure food access
- 2.2: End malnutrition in all forms
- 2.3: Double agricultural productivity of small-scale farmers
- 2.4: Ensure sustainable food production systems
- 2.5: Maintain genetic diversity of crops and livestock
