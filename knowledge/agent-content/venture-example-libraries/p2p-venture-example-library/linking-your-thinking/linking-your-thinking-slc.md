## Canvas Structure

### Purpose
Solving information overwhelm through flexible knowledge management systems that transform passive note-taking into active thinking and sensemaking.

### Impact
Society-wide adoption of effective knowledge management creating more thoughtful, creative, and collaborative knowledge workers.
*[See full Impact Model](linking-your-thinking-impact-model.md)*

-----
### Customer Model

#### Solution
Community-driven workshops, digital vaults, and lifetime learning access combining live instruction with practical knowledge management frameworks.

### Customer Model
#### Customers
**Customer Types** 
**Users** - Globally distributed English speaking online technology users, with middle to upper-middle class incomes

**Customer Segments**
1. **Knowledge Workers:** Professional creators, researchers, and consultants managing complex information across projects
2. **Lifelong Learners:** Individuals with 10+ years PKM experience seeking anxiety-reducing, advanced methodologies
3. **PKM Enthusiasts:** Existing Obsidian users and methodology practitioners wanting more flexible frameworks

**Early Adopters:**
- Knowledge workers overwhelmed by existing systems and early adopters of PKM methodologies seeking flexible alternatives to rigid frameworks

#### Jobs to be Done

- Transform scattered information into reliable creative output and professional insights through organized thinking
- Replace overwhelming note collections with anxiety-reducing systems that actually support meaningful work
- Bridge methodological gaps with flexible frameworks that enhance rather than constrain natural thinking



**Existing Alternatives:**

- BASB, corporate productivity systems, traditional folder-based tools (Evernote, OneNote, Notion)
- Basic note-taking apps, failed attempts at rigid methodologies, overwhelming digital hoarding
- Zettelkasten, Roam Research, basic Obsidian setups, competing methodology courses

#### Unique Value Proposition

- Flexible frameworks that mirror natural thinking while enabling reliable creative output and insight generation
- Joy-focused systems that reduce anxiety while building sustainable, personally-tailored knowledge management approaches  
- Innovative MOCs methodology with community-driven learning and advanced Obsidian integration

#### Solution
Community-driven workshops, digital vaults, and lifetime learning access combining live instruction with practical knowledge management frameworks.

-----
### Economic Model

#### Channels
YouTube education, alumni referrals, PKM community engagement, conference speaking, and value-first free resources.

#### Costs
Personnel, technology infrastructure, content development, and marketing with alumni mentors reducing traditional staffing costs.

#### Revenue
Premium workshops ($997-$1,697), digital products ($199-$497), and recurring subscriptions with high-margin digital delivery.

#### Advantage
Founder authority, community network effects, unique methodology positioning, premium pricing power, and scalable alumni mentor systems.

------
#### Key Metrics
1,600+ alumni, 60+ countries, sustained engagement, transformational testimonials, and long-term behavior persistence metrics.
