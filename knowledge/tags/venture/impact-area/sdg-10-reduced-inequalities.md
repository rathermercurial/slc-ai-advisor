# sdg-10-reduced-inequalities

## Definition
The `sdg-10-reduced-inequalities` tag identifies ventures focused on reducing inequality within and among countries, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 10.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Address income and social inequalities
- Promote social inclusion and equity
- Support marginalized communities
- Focus on equal opportunity and access
- Address systemic discrimination

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `employment-model`, `cross-subsidy`, `policy-advocacy`
