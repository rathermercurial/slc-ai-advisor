# air

## Definition
The `air` tag identifies ventures focused on air quality, pollution reduction, and atmospheric environmental impacts, based on IRIS+ impact themes.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `pollution`, `clean-energy`
