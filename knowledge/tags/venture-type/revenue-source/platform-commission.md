# platform-commission

## Definition
The `platform-commission` tag identifies ventures that generate revenue from marketplace take-rates and facilitating transactions between parties.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Operate marketplace platforms
- Generate revenue from transaction fees
- Facilitate exchanges between buyers and sellers
- Take percentage commissions on transactions

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `revenue-source`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `revenue-source`
- Often paired with: `ict`, `financial-services`
- May combine with: `product-service-impact`
