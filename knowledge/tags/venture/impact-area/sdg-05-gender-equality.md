# sdg-05-gender-equality

## Definition
The `sdg-05-gender-equality` tag identifies ventures focused on achieving gender equality and empowering all women and girls, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 5.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Address gender-based discrimination and inequality
- Empower women and girls economically and socially
- Support women's leadership and participation
- Focus on gender-based violence prevention
- Promote women's rights and gender equity

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`
- Often combined with employment model or capacity building mechanisms
