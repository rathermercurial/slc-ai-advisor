## Canvas Structure

### Purpose
Make responsible disposal of hard-to-recycle household items simple and accessible while reducing landfill waste.

### Impact
A future where responsible disposal is as convenient as trash, supporting a circular economy with minimal waste.
*[See full Impact Model](ridwell-impact-model.md)*

-----
### Customer Model

#### Solution
Subscription doorstep pickup service with provided bags, bi-weekly collection, and processing through vetted partners.

### Customer Model
#### Customers
Environmentally-conscious households seeking convenient solutions for hard-to-recycle household items.

**Early Adopters:**
Environmental activists and sustainability-focused families in Seattle neighborhoods (Queen Anne, Ballard, West Seattle, Capitol Hill have nearly 20% participation)

#### Jobs to be Done
Reduce environmental guilt while maintaining convenient household waste disposal without time-intensive research.

**Existing Alternatives:**
Throwing items in trash, driving to specialized recyclers, or indefinite storage while procrastinating disposal.

#### Unique Value Proposition
Guilt-free environmental impact through convenient scheduled doorstep pickup with full transparency about item destinations.

#### Solution
Subscription doorstep pickup service with provided bags, bi-weekly collection, and processing through vetted partners.

-----
### Economic Model

#### Channels
Digital marketing and referrals for acquisition; direct subscription service with proprietary logistics for distribution.

#### Costs
Logistics operations, warehouse facilities, partner transportation, technology platform, and customer acquisition costs.

#### Revenue
Monthly subscription fees ($14-24) plus add-on services, totaling recurring revenue from 90,000+ members.

#### Advantage
First-mover advantage, extensive partner network, proven logistics capabilities, strong environmental brand, and customer loyalty.

------
#### Key Metrics
Member retention, monthly recurring revenue, pounds diverted, contamination rates, partner capacity, and customer satisfaction.
