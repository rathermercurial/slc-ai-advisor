# subscription

## Definition
The `subscription` tag identifies ventures that generate revenue through recurring payments for ongoing delivery of digital or physical products and services.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Provide recurring digital or physical deliveries
- Generate revenue from subscription models
- Offer ongoing access to products or services
- Follow recurring payment structures

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `revenue-source`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `revenue-source`
- Often paired with: `ict`, `education`, `media`
- May combine with: `product-service-impact`
