## Definition
The `costs` tag identifies content about major ongoing operational expenses required to deliver customer value and create impact - understanding cost structure for sustainable operations.

For costs, this might include the costs involved with making your product or service, marketing costs, distribution costs, and the cost of delivering your impact activities. This will also include any other major ongoing costs that your business model will have to cover. Keep all of this simple and high level to start with. This will give you the shape of your financial model and allow you to assess its viability as well as what might need to change about it. Over time, you will add in more detail as you move forward with your enterprise and test the revenue and cost assumptions in this model.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Defines cost structure analysis and optimization
- Discusses cost management strategies for social enterprises
- Provides frameworks for sustainable cost models
- Analyzes cost-effectiveness and efficiency approaches

## Always Include When
- Content focuses on ongoing operational expense management
- Cost structure optimization frameworks
- Cost-effectiveness analysis methodologies
- Sustainable cost model development

## Never Include When
- One-time investment or startup costs without ongoing cost focus
- Revenue content (use `revenue`)
