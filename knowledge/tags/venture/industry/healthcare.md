# healthcare

## Definition
The `healthcare` tag identifies ventures operating in the medical services, health products, wellness, and healthcare technology sectors.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Provide medical or clinical services
- Develop health-related products or technologies
- Focus on wellness and preventive care
- Address healthcare access and affordability
- Operate in health technology and digital health
- Support healthcare system strengthening

## Always Include When
- Medical clinics and healthcare facilities
- Health technology and digital health platforms
- Medical device and pharmaceutical companies
- Wellness and preventive care services
- Healthcare capacity building initiatives
- Telemedicine and remote health services

## Never Include When
- General wellness products without clear health focus
- Fitness and exercise not focused on health outcomes
- Food and nutrition ventures (use `food-beverage` unless health-focused)
- Educational content not specifically health-related

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `industry`
- Often combined with health-related impact area tags
- May combine with various revenue and funding source tags

## Content Examples
- Community health clinics in underserved areas
- Telemedicine platforms for rural populations
- Medical devices for developing countries
- Health worker training and capacity building
- Maternal and child health programs
- Mental health and wellness services

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `industry`
- Impact alignment: `sdg-03-good-health-and-well-being`, `health`
- Revenue sources: `service-fees`, `product-sales`, `gov-contract`
- Impact mechanisms: `direct-service`, `product-service-impact`, `capacity-building`

## Industry Segments
- **Primary Care**: Basic healthcare and family medicine
- **Specialty Care**: Specialized medical services
- **Digital Health**: Technology-enabled healthcare solutions
- **Medical Devices**: Healthcare equipment and devices
- **Pharmaceuticals**: Drug development and distribution
- **Wellness**: Preventive care and wellness services

## Key Considerations
- Regulatory compliance and healthcare standards
- Patient safety and clinical efficacy
- Healthcare access and affordability
- Quality of care and health outcomes
- Healthcare system integration
- Professional healthcare delivery standards
