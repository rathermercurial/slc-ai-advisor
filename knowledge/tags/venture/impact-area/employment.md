# employment

## Definition
The `employment` tag identifies ventures focused on job creation, workforce development, and employment opportunities, based on IRIS+ impact themes.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `sdg-08-decent-work-and-economic-growth`, `employment-model`
