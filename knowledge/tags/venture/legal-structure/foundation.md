# foundation

## Definition
The `foundation` tag identifies ventures operating as private or public foundation structures focused on grantmaking or direct charitable activities.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Operate as foundation structures
- Focus on grantmaking activities
- Have endowment-based operations
- Engage in charitable purposes

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `legal-structure`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `legal-structure`
- Often paired with: `grant`, `donation` funding sources
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
