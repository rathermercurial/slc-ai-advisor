# policy-advocacy

## Definition
The `policy-advocacy` tag identifies ventures that create impact by working to change policies, regulations, laws, or institutional practices that affect the target issue.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Advocate for policy changes and reforms
- Work to influence institutional practices
- Focus on regulatory and legal changes
- Address systemic issues through advocacy

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-mechanism`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-mechanism`
- Often paired with: `systems-change`
- May combine with: various impact areas
