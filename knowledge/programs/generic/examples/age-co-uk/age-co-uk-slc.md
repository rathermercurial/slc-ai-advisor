# Age Co UK - Social Lean Canvas

## Canvas Overview
---
**Venture:** Age Co UK
**Date:** 2025-07-02
**Version:** 1.0
---

## Canvas Structure

### Purpose
Help older people live independently and safely while funding Age UK's charitable work through profitable later-life services.

### Impact
A society where every older person lives with dignity, security, and support rather than vulnerability and exclusion.
*[See full Impact Model](age-co-uk-impact-model.md)*

### Customer Model

#### Jobs to be Done
Achieve peace of mind in later life through independence, financial protection, and trusted guidance that serves a purpose.

**Existing Alternatives:**
For-profit providers like Saga, mainstream insurers with age restrictions, or going without essential services and making do.

#### Solution
One-stop later-life services: over-50s insurance, personal alarms, mobility aids, and care products via trusted expert partners.

#### Unique Value Proposition
Reliable age-friendly solutions where every purchase supports Age UK's charity work - quality service with social impact.

#### Advantage
Decades of Age UK brand trust plus mission-driven value proposition where customers get service and contribute good.

#### Customers
Older adults (50+) seeking age-tailored services for themselves, plus family caregivers arranging solutions for aging relatives.

**Early Adopters:**
Age UK supporters who trusted the brand to meet needs mainstream providers weren't addressing effectively.

### Economic Model

#### Key Metrics
Multi-million annual turnover enabling £3-4 million yearly donations to Age UK, serving thousands across insurance and mobility.

#### Channels
Age UK's website and materials, direct mail, daytime TV/radio ads, plus phone services and professional home installations.

#### Costs
Partner payments for services, marketing to reach seniors, plus operational costs for staff, customer support, and compliance.

#### Revenue
Trading commissions and fees from insurance policies and later-life products, with income from every customer purchase.


Please note, this business model has been compiled from publicly available data. It is a best estimate of this organization's impact model but information may be out of date and/or could differ from the organization's own description of their business model.
