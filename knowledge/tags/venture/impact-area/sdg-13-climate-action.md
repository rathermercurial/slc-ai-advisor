# sdg-13-climate-action

## Definition
The `sdg-13-climate-action` tag identifies ventures focused on taking urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 13.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `clean-energy`, `climate`, `policy-advocacy`
