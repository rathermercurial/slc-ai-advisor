# cic

## Definition
The `cic` tag identifies ventures operating as Community Interest Companies - a UK legal structure combining limited company flexibility with social purpose requirements and asset lock.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Have CIC legal status (UK-specific)
- Operate with asset lock provisions
- Focus on community purpose
- Have regulated dividend distributions

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `legal-structure`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `legal-structure`
- Often paired with: various funding sources
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
- Note: UK-specific legal structure
