# report

## Definition
The `report` tag identifies content that provides comprehensive, analytical documents that synthesize information, insights, and findings related to the Social Lean Canvas framework, its application, or the broader social venture ecosystem.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Provides comprehensive analysis and synthesis of information
- Offers research findings and insights across multiple ventures or topics
- Includes systematic evaluation and assessment of trends or patterns
- Contains structured documentation of findings and recommendations

## Always Include When
- Content presents comprehensive analysis across multiple data points
- Systematic research findings and insights
- Structured documentation with findings and recommendations
- Multi-venture or ecosystem-level analysis
- Performance assessments and evaluation reports
- Trend analysis and pattern recognition documents

## Never Include When
- Single venture analysis (use `case-study` instead)
- Brief summaries or overviews (use `resource` or `concept`)
- Simple documentation without analytical depth
- Personal reflections or opinions without systematic analysis

## Required Combinations
- Often combined with relevant canvas section tags when reports focus on specific framework elements
- May include venture stage tags when reports analyze stage-specific patterns
- Should include venture-type tags when reports focus on specific venture characteristics
- Often paired with `concept` for theoretical framework application in reports

## Content Examples
- Impact measurement and evaluation reports
- Social venture ecosystem analysis
- Framework effectiveness studies
- Sector-specific venture performance analysis
- Regional or market assessment reports
- Longitudinal studies of venture development
- Comparative analysis of framework applications

## Related Tags
- May combine with any canvas section tags for section-focused reports
- Often includes venture stage tags for stage-specific analysis
- Frequently paired with venture-type tags for targeted analysis
- May appear with `case-study` for reports that include detailed venture examples
- Often combined with `resource` when reports serve as reference materials

## Report Characteristics
- Systematic methodology and approach
- Data-driven analysis and conclusions
- Clear structure with executive summary, findings, and recommendations
- Evidence-based insights and patterns
- Actionable recommendations and implications
- Proper documentation of sources and methodology
- Visual representations of data and findings where appropriate

## Quality Criteria
- Comprehensive coverage of the stated scope
- Rigorous analytical methodology
- Clear, actionable findings and recommendations
- Balanced perspective acknowledging limitations
- Educational and practical value for framework users
- Professional documentation standards
- Reproducible analysis approach
