# financial-services

## Definition
The `financial-services` tag identifies ventures focused on financial inclusion, access to finance, and financial services, based on IRIS+ impact themes.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `financial-services` industry tag
