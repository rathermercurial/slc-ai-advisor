## Impact Chain

### Issue
Autistic adults face systemic unemployment despite capabilities—Auticon bridges this employment gap.

### Participants
Autistic consultants, coaches, clients, and advocates co-create social impact through inclusive employment.

### Activities
Hire autistic professionals, deliver tech services, support inclusion, and educate clients.

### Short-Term Outcomes
Consultants employed and supported; clients gain quality results and inclusion awareness.

### Medium-Term Outcomes
Consultants grow in careers; clients adopt neuroinclusive practices; model scales globally.

### Long-Term Outcomes
Autism employment gap narrows; neurodiversity embraced in business and culture.

### Impact
Inclusive workplaces where autistic talent thrives and neurodiversity is celebrated.
