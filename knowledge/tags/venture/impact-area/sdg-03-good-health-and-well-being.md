# sdg-03-good-health-and-well-being

## Definition
The `sdg-03-good-health-and-well-being` tag identifies ventures focused on ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for all at all ages, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 3.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Provide healthcare services and access
- Develop health technologies and solutions
- Address disease prevention and treatment
- Focus on maternal and child health
- Support mental health and wellness initiatives

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`
- Often combined with healthcare industry tag
- May combine with direct service or product-service impact mechanisms

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `healthcare`, `direct-service`, `product-service-impact`
