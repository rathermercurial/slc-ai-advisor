# capacity-building

## Definition
The `capacity-building` tag identifies ventures that create impact by building skills, knowledge, capabilities, and organizational strength in individuals or communities.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Build skills and capabilities in target populations
- Provide training and development programs
- Strengthen organizational capacity
- Focus on knowledge transfer and capability development

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-mechanism`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-mechanism`
- Often paired with: `education`, `employment`
- May combine with: `service-fees`, `grant`
