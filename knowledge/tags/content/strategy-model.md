# strategy-model

## Definition
The `strategy-model` tag identifies content about strategic approaches and frameworks that social enterprises use to achieve their goals, providing tools for strategic planning, decision-making, and organizational development.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Explains strategic frameworks and methodologies
- Discusses strategic planning and decision-making approaches
- Provides organizational development and strategic guidance
- Analyzes strategic effectiveness and implementation

## Always Include When
- Content covers strategic frameworks and methodologies
- Strategic planning and implementation guidance
- Organizational strategy and development approaches
- Strategic analysis and evaluation

## Never Include When
- Operational or tactical content without strategic context
- Canvas section content (use canvas section tags)
- General business content not focused on strategic frameworks

## Required Combinations
- Often combined with specific strategy sub-component tags
- May include canvas section tags when strategy relates to specific canvas areas

## Content Examples
- Strategic planning frameworks
- Organizational development methodologies
- Strategic decision-making approaches
- Strategic implementation and evaluation

## Related Tags
- Sub-component tags: `business-model-strategy`, `organizational-strategy`, `capital-strategy`, `strategy-diagram`
- Often appears with canvas section tags for strategic canvas approaches
- May combine with `venture-stage-model` for stage-specific strategies

## Key Concepts
