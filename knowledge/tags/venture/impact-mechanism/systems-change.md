# systems-change

## Definition
The `systems-change` tag identifies ventures that create impact by addressing root causes and changing underlying systems that perpetuate social or environmental problems.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Address root causes of social problems
- Work to change underlying systems and structures
- Focus on transforming institutional norms
- Alter market structures and incentive systems

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-mechanism`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-mechanism`
- Often paired with: `policy-advocacy`, `research-innovation`
- May combine with: various impact areas
