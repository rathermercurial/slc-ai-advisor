# charity

## Definition
The `charity` tag identifies ventures operating as registered charitable organizations with tax-exempt status, dedicated to public benefit purposes.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Have registered charity status
- Operate with tax-exempt benefits
- Focus on public benefit purposes
- Meet charitable organization requirements

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `legal-structure`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `legal-structure`
- Often paired with: `grant`, `donation` funding sources
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
