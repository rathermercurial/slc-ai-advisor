# climate

## Definition
The `climate` tag identifies ventures focused on climate change mitigation, adaptation, and resilience, based on IRIS+ impact themes.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `sdg-13-climate-action`, `clean-energy`
