# reinvest-surplus

## Definition
The `reinvest-surplus` tag identifies ventures that create impact by channeling financial surpluses (profits) back into social programs and impact activities.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Reinvest profits into social programs
- Channel surplus revenue into impact activities
- Use profit reinvestment as impact mechanism
- Commit to using financial returns for social good

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-mechanism`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-mechanism`
- Often paired with: various revenue sources
- May combine with: various impact areas
