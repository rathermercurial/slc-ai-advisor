# early-stage

## Definition
The `early-stage` tag identifies content specifically relevant to early-stage social enterprises that are building initial solutions, testing with early adopters, and validating core assumptions.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Addresses challenges specific to early-stage ventures
- Provides guidance for early-stage canvas completion
- Discusses early-stage validation approaches
- Analyzes early-stage case studies and examples

## Always Include When
- Content specifically targets early-stage venture needs
- Early-stage canvas completion guidance
- Early adopter validation methodologies
- Early-stage business model development

## Never Include When
- Content applies equally to all venture stages
- Content is specific to other stages (idea, growth, scale)
- General business content not tied to venture stage

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-stage-model`
- Often combined with specific canvas section tags for stage-specific guidance

## Content Examples
- Early-stage canvas templates and examples
- Early adopter validation frameworks
- Early-stage impact measurement approaches
- Early-stage funding and resource strategies

## Related Tags
- Parent tag: `venture-stage-model`
- Often appears with `early-adopters` (focus on early customers)
- May combine with `assumption-risk` (high uncertainty in early stage)
- Often paired with `experiment-test` for validation approaches

## Stage Characteristics
- Building initial solutions
- Testing with early adopters
- Validating core assumptions
- Initial canvas completion
- High uncertainty and iteration
- Resource constraints
- Proof of concept development
