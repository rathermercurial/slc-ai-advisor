# nonprofit-inc

## Definition
The `nonprofit-inc` tag identifies ventures operating as incorporated nonprofit organizations that may or may not have charitable status but operate for social purposes.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Have nonprofit incorporation status
- Operate for social rather than profit purposes
- May have tax advantages
- Have restrictions on profit distribution

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `legal-structure`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `legal-structure`
- Often paired with: `grant`, `donation` funding sources
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
