# activities

## Definition
The `activities` tag identifies content about specific actions and interventions undertaken by the enterprise to address the issue and create desired outputs in the impact model.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Defines activity design and planning approaches
- Discusses implementation methodologies
- Provides frameworks for activity effectiveness
- Analyzes activity-outcome relationships

## Always Include When
- Content focuses on implementation planning and activity design
- Activity effectiveness measurement approaches
- Implementation methodology frameworks
- Activity-output relationship analysis

## Never Include When
- General business operations not tied to impact
- Administrative activities without impact connection

## Required Combinations
- Must include appropriate canvas-sections hierarchy: [impact-model, activities]

## Content Examples
- Activity design frameworks
- Implementation planning methodologies
- Activity effectiveness measurement
- Activity-outcome logic models

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: [impact-model](../impact-model.md)
- Often appears with [participants](../participants/participants.md) (who engages in activities)
- Often paired with [outputs](../outputs/outputs.md) (activities produce outputs)
- May combine with solution (activities implement solution)

## Key Concepts
- Theory-based activity design
- Activity sequence and timing
- Resource requirements
- Activity monitoring
- Participant engagement in activities
- Activity adaptation and iteration
