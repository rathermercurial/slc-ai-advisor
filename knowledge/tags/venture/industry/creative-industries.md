# creative-industries

## Definition
The `creative-industries` tag identifies ventures in arts, media, entertainment, design, and creative content production.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Operate in arts and creative sectors
- Produce media and entertainment content
- Provide design and creative services
- Support creative community development

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `industry`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `industry`
- Often paired with: `capacity-building`, `employment-model`
- May combine with: various impact areas
