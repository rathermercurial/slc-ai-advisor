# waste

## Definition
The `waste` tag identifies ventures focused on waste management, reduction, recycling, and circular economy approaches, based on IRIS+ impact themes.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `circular-economy`, `sdg-12-responsible-consumption-and-production`
