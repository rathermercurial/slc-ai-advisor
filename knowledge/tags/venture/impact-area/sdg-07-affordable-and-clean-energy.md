# sdg-07-affordable-and-clean-energy

## Definition
The `sdg-07-affordable-and-clean-energy` tag identifies ventures focused on ensuring access to affordable, reliable, sustainable and modern energy for all, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 7.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Provide access to affordable clean energy
- Develop renewable energy technologies
- Address energy poverty and access
- Focus on energy efficiency solutions
- Support sustainable energy infrastructure

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`
- Often combined with clean-energy industry tag
