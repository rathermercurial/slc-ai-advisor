# grant

## Definition
The `grant` tag identifies ventures that receive traditional grant funding from foundations, government agencies, development organizations, or other institutional grantmakers.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Receive grant funding from foundations or government
- Operate with project-specific grant support
- Have non-repayable funding arrangements
- Work with institutional grantmakers

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `funding-source`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `funding-source`
- Often paired with: `charity`, `nonprofit-inc` legal structures
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
