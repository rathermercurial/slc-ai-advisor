# impact

## Definition
The `impact` tag identifies content about the ultimate social or environmental change that emerges from long-term outcomes - the fundamental transformation the enterprise aims to achieve through its purpose.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Defines ultimate impact vision and measurement
- Discusses long-term social/environmental change
- Provides frameworks for impact assessment
- Analyzes impact realization and attribution

## Always Include When
- Content focuses on ultimate change vision
- Impact statement development and articulation
- Long-term social/environmental transformation
- Impact measurement and evaluation approaches

## Never Include When
- Short or medium-term outcome content (use specific outcome tags)
- Output measurement (use `outputs`)
- General impact measurement not about ultimate impact

## Required Combinations
- Must include appropriate canvas-sections hierarchy: [impact-model, impact]

## Content Examples
- Impact statement development frameworks
- Ultimate impact assessment methodologies
- Long-term transformation measurement approaches
- Impact vision articulation methods
- Long-term impact case studies

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: [impact-model](../impact-model.md)
- Often appears with purpose (impact realizes purpose)
- Often paired with [long-term-outcomes](../long-term-outcomes/long-term-outcomes.md) (emerges from long-term outcomes)

## Key Concepts
- Ultimate social/environmental change
- Systemic transformation
- Generational impact
- Attribution challenges
- Impact vision and theory
- Long-term change measurement
