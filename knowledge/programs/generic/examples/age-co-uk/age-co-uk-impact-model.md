## Impact Chain

### Issue
Millions of older people face loneliness, mobility challenges, and financial insecurity without adequate support from traditional services.

### Participants
Older people, Age UK staff/volunteers, family caregivers, and trusted partner companies delivering insurance and mobility solutions.

### Activities
Providing tailored products and support to seniors while gifting profits to fund Age UK's advice helplines and befriending programs.

### Short-Term Outcomes
Customers gain instant peace of mind through safety products and fair insurance, while Age UK receives funding for expanded services.

### Medium-Term Outcomes
More seniors live independently at home with financial stability, while Age UK programs deliver measurable well-being improvements.

### Long-Term Outcomes
Thousands age with dignity and security, with stronger community safety nets and positive shifts in aging attitudes.

### Impact
A society where every older person lives with dignity, security, and support rather than vulnerability and exclusion.
