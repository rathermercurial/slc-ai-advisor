# trust

## Definition
The `trust` tag identifies ventures operating as legal trust structures where assets are held for specific social or charitable purposes.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Operate under trust legal structure
- Have trustee governance arrangements
- Focus on asset protection for social purposes
- Serve specific beneficiaries

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `legal-structure`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `legal-structure`
- Often paired with: `grant`, `donation` funding sources
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
