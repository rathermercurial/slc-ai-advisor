# water-sanitation

## Definition
The `water-sanitation` tag identifies ventures focused on water access, treatment, sanitation services, and water management.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Provide water access and treatment solutions
- Develop sanitation services and systems
- Focus on water management and conservation
- Create water and sanitation technologies

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `industry`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `industry`
- Often paired with: `sdg-06-clean-water-and-sanitation`, `water`
- May combine with: `product-service-impact`, `direct-service`
