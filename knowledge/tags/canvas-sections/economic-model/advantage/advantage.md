# advantage

## Definition
The `advantage` tag identifies content about specific competitive advantages that enable the enterprise to sustain impact and operations over time - what makes the approach defensible and unique.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Defines competitive advantage identification and development
- Discusses sustainable advantage strategies
- Provides frameworks for advantage analysis
- Analyzes competitive positioning and differentiation

## Always Include When
- Content focuses on competitive advantage development
- Differentiation and positioning strategies
- Sustainable advantage frameworks
- Competitive moat development

## Never Include When
- General competitive analysis without advantage focus
- Operational improvements without competitive advantage impact

## Required Combinations
- Must include appropriate canvas-sections hierarchy: [economic-model, advantage]

## Content Examples
- Competitive advantage frameworks and analysis
- Strategic positioning methodologies
- Advantage sustainability approaches
- Competitive positioning and differentiation strategies

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: [economic-model](../economic-model.md)
- Often appears with `concept` and leverage for leverage-based advantages
- May combine with impact-model tags (impact as advantage)
- Often paired with solution (solution creates advantage)

## Key Concepts
- Sustainable competitive advantages
- Network effects and platforms
- Impact-driven differentiation
- Mission-driven advantage
- Resource-based advantages
- Strategic positioning
