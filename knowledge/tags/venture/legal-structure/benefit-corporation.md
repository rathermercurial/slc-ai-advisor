# benefit-corporation

## Definition
The `benefit-corporation` tag identifies ventures operating as for-profit corporations legally required to consider social and environmental impact alongside financial returns.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Have benefit corporation legal status
- Must consider stakeholder impacts
- Required to report on social/environmental performance
- Balance profit with purpose legally

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `legal-structure`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `legal-structure`
- Often paired with: `impact-equity` funding source
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
