# concessional-debt

## Definition
The `concessional-debt` tag identifies ventures that receive below-market-rate loans or debt financing with favorable terms designed to support social ventures.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Receive below-market interest rate loans
- Have flexible repayment terms
- Access patient debt capital
- Use social impact-focused debt financing

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `funding-source`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `funding-source`
- Often paired with: hybrid or for-profit legal structures
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
