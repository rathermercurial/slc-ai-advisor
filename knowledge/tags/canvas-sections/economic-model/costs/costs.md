# costs

## Definition
The `costs` tag identifies content about major ongoing operational expenses required to deliver customer value and create impact - understanding cost structure for sustainable operations.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to content that:
- Defines cost structure analysis and optimization
- Discusses cost management strategies for social enterprises
- Provides frameworks for sustainable cost models
- Analyzes cost-effectiveness and efficiency approaches

## Always Include When
- Content focuses on ongoing operational expense management
- Cost structure optimization frameworks
- Cost-effectiveness analysis methodologies
- Sustainable cost model development

## Never Include When
- One-time investment or startup costs without ongoing cost focus
- Revenue content (use `revenue`)

## Required Combinations
- Must include appropriate canvas-sections hierarchy: [economic-model, costs]

## Content Examples
- Cost structure analysis frameworks
- Cost optimization strategies for social enterprises
- Impact cost analysis and measurement
- Cost-effectiveness evaluation methodologies

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: [economic-model](../economic-model.md)
- Often appears with [revenue](../revenue/revenue.md) (cost-revenue relationships)
- Often paired with [financial-model](../financial-model/financial-model.md) (costs in financial projections)

## Key Concepts
- Fixed vs. variable costs
- Cost structure optimization
- Impact cost measurement
- Cost per outcome/impact
- Economies of scale
- Social enterprise cost challenges
