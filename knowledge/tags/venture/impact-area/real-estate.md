# real-estate

## Definition
The `real-estate` tag identifies ventures focused on housing, real estate development, and property-related impacts, based on IRIS+ impact themes.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `housing`, `sdg-11-sustainable-cities-and-communities`
