# energy

## Definition
The `energy` tag identifies ventures focused on energy access, renewable energy, and energy systems, based on IRIS+ impact themes.

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `impact-area`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `impact-area`
- Often paired with: `sdg-07-affordable-and-clean-energy`, `clean-energy`
