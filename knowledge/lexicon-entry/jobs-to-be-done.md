# Jobs to be Done

**Quick Definition**: Core tasks or needs motivating customers to engage with your solution - what customers "hire" your product or service to do for them.

**Key Insight**: Go deeper than obvious needs - dig for the particular thing you can do that isn't currently available from existing alternatives.
