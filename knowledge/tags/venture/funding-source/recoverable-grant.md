# recoverable-grant

## Definition
The `recoverable-grant` tag identifies ventures that receive grant funding with potential repayment requirements based on venture success or achievement of specific milestones.

## Usage Criteria
Apply this tag to ventures that:
- Receive grants with repayment contingencies
- Have success-based repayment requirements
- Operate with patient capital arrangements
- Use hybrid grant-loan structures

## Required Combinations
- Must include `venture-type` and `funding-source`

## Related Tags
- Parent tags: `venture-type`, `funding-source`
- Often paired with: hybrid legal structures
- May combine with: various impact mechanisms
